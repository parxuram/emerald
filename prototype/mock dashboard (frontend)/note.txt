Note: The frontend-integrated version is currently under active optimization and does not yet achieve the same detection consistency as the standalone prototype. This is primarily due to frontend-oriented performance optimizations, including reduced inference resolution and additional filtering mechanisms introduced to improve processing speed and responsiveness. While these changes enhance runtime efficiency, they can reduce the detection accuracy of smaller or distant objects, particularly motorcycles. Further tuning is being performed to balance real-time performance with detection accuracy and to align frontend results with those obtained during backend testing.
